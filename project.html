About Our Project

Chick Counterweight Monitoring System

This project proposes the development of an automatic chicks counterweight system designed to measure the weight of chicks accurately and efficiently. The system aims to reduce human error and labor involved in manual weighing by introducing a sensor-based digital solution. Using a load cell integrated with a microcontroller, the system can provide real-time weight readings on a display. This innovation helps farmers monitor chick growth performance more effectively, ensuring better feed management and early detection of growth irregularities. The project contributes to improving efficiency and productivity in poultry management.

Introduction

The poultry industry plays a vital role in food production, and monitoring chick growth is essential to ensure their healthy development. Traditionally, weighing chicks is performed manually using mechanical or digital scales, which is time-consuming and often stressful for the animals. Manual weighing also increases the likelihood of human error, making it difficult to track accurate growth data. To address these challenges, an automatic chicks counterweight system is proposed to measure and record chick weights efficiently and consistently.
